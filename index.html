The Warrior's Stone

The war with the Serken is finally turning to the favor of the Allied worlds for the first time in over a decade, yet it is far from over. Lt. Commander Roy O’Hara leads his squadron of combat fighters against the enemy’s latest Super Destroyer and is shot down over an unexplored planet.

The Last Flight of the Phoenix

Different paths of unlikely allies and new foes will intersect in the skies and on the ground of New Terra once again, where everything will change and destinies will collide.

The Prophet's Stone

Time will not be our ally. The evil that comes will not be a simple beast or monster. We must all be ready, especially you.

Lt. Reilly 2321

Jack Reilly’s luck ran out just before the war did. Released on a medical discharge shortly after the end of the war with the Serkins, he went out into the galaxy looking for a job...

Lt. Reilly and the Black Bird Offensive

He took the job that Mitchell offered him in the Military Investigations office on New Harmony, got his commission reinstated, and finally had a chance to figure out his relationship with Kayla. Yet one of his cases turned out to be much, much bigger than he expected it to be.

Lt. Reilly and the Phantom Raptor

Just because the war was over doesn’t mean Reilly didn’t have battles to fight and challenges to face. Back on New Harmony, he settled into a domestic life with Kayla and continued to work with Major Mitchell to flush out corruption within the service. Their latest case involving a drug-smuggling turned out to be much bigger than they could have ever expected, leading them to a missing prototype starship and the hunt for the pirates who snatched it.

About the Author

Matthew O. Duncan is part of the resurgence of Old School Sci-fi. His books are a mix of Military Sci-fi, Fantasy, Romance, and Mystery weaved together with non-stop action.
